What's Kokong?


Kokong is an art project made by Kjetil Skaro (me), a young man from Nesodden, Norway. I do analog and digital photography, drawings with mostly ink & watercolor, but mainly i play, write, produce and mix music. I'm also a skateboarder who is heavily influenced by the culture and everything that surrounds it. I hope you find something that you like on this website!


If you got any questions and/or would like to buy a piece of art, please contact me at: kjetilskaro@gmail.com.
