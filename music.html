Some of my musical projects

Animal Kingdom


Crispy Carrot is a musical experiment existing in a psychedelic ambient-acoustic universe with influences from genres like folk, rock, soul, jazz, reggae and many other sorts of music.

All songs are written, recorded and mixed by Kjetil Skaro at beautiful Nesodden, Norway. Mastered by Njål Frode Lie, NFL Productions at Hydroparken Studios, Notodden. Artwork by Kjetil Skaro. Special thanks to Viktor Skårnes playing the drums on "Superman" and bass-guitar on "Frostnatt". Also a huge thanks to friends and family. Love you all.
